Want to show a few photos to friends or family online ? Photozzap is a web app which turns photo viewing into an interactive experience, allowing you to see what photos your friends are looking at, and take turns in controlling the slideshow.

Features

See what Photozzap does differently

Interactive Photo Viewing

View photos together, in realtime. Use the Look Here or Follow Me buttons to show friends the photo you are currently looking at. You can see which photo they are looking at in the Users tab.

Instant Photo Gallery

Create a photo gallery faster than any other site out there. Just type your nickname, the gallery name, click OK and start uploading pictures. Upload from Dropbox, Flickr, Facebook and Instagram. No login needed.

Desktop, Tablet and Mobile Friendly

Create or view photos on your desktop PC/Mac, iPad, iPhone or Android phone. The interface and photos are resized based on which device you are using.

Privacy Oriented

No registration needed. Private by default. Only people who have the URL can access your pictures. Pictures don't stay online forever, you can remove them immediately, or they get deleted after 7 days.
